Cut Obama

I cut Barack and Michelle Obama at the wedding (this or this), using photo2cnccut, on 100x100 mm aluminum plate.

I wanted to try a more difficult subject, and I think it turned out relatively fine.

I can recognize features of those small (and beautiful) faces.

The large black and white areas are not so unintelligible as I had thought. (the depth-adjustment on machine was a bit hard, though).

For painting and finishing, there is room for improvement. I'm not yet certain which is the best (re-cutting, sanding or polishing? mat white or mirror finish?).
